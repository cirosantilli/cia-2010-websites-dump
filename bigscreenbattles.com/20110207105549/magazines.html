Publications & Online Magazines

  • Boxoffice Magazine - Online version of the print magazine. Film reviews, news, industry classifieds, back issues, subscription information, and links.
  • Cashiers du Cinemart Online - Online appendix to the infamous Detroit film zine. Kooky news, interviews, and movie reviews.
  • Cineaste Magazine - Online version of the magazine focusing on the art and politics of cinema. Highlights of current and upcoming issues, subscription information, contributors, and links.
  • D+CFilm - Covering film and filmmaking-related news in Devon and Cornwall, UK. News, feature articles, resources, festival coverage, and community.
  • Eclipse Magazine - Reviews, polls, trailers and interviews.
  • eFilmCritic.com - Australia's movie magazine offering movie reviews by visitors to the site.
  • Empire Online - Online version of the print magazine provides news, reviews, feature articles, discussion groups, archives, search by title or text, and subscription details.
  • Errata - A collection of essays, reviews, commentary, and discussion about cinema.
  • EyeForFilm - UK-based online film magazine with news, reviews, feature articles, and festival coverage.
  • Film Comment Magazine - Readers of the magazine will find articles on films old and new, foreign and domestic, narrative and documentary.
  • Film West Magazine - Informative journal of Irish cinema including reviews, news, interviews from the world of film.
  • Filmink Magazine - Film news and gossip, movie reviews, video and DVD releases, Australian and international film industry information.
  • Films in Review - Online version of the oldest film magazine in the United States. Background information, reviews, interviews, feature articles, as well as home entertainment reviews.
  • Hollywood North Magazine - Publication focusing on filmmaking in Canada. Film and DVD reviews, awards, industry announcements, image gallery, multimedia, message board, and links.
  • Inside Out Film - Ezine with filmmaking resources, movie reviews, festival and local film news coverage, and links.
  • Internet Video Magazine - Tutorials, equipment descriptions, product reviews, featured internet films and videos.
  • 15 Minutes - An interview magazine, featuring interviews with actors, writers, and directors from the world of film in RealVideo format.
  • Movie Magazine International - Weekly, nationally-syndicated radio show provides a look at the film scene, including movie reviews, weekly trivia contests, and interviews with actors and directors.
  • Movie Memories Magazine - Dedicated to The Golden Age of films, film stars and directors both in Hollywood and the UK, in the 1940s, 50s and 60s. Subscription information, back issues for sale, reader comments, and a sample issue.
  • The Moving Arts Film Journal - Features news, reviews, commentary, and a podcast.
  • Moving Pictures Magazine - Online version of the print magazine. Film, DVD, book, and soundtrack reviews, feature articles, interviews, multimedia, festival coverage, filmmaker profiles, filmmaking resources, and a section devoted to short film.
  • Obsessed With FIlm - Reviews, feature articles, and discussion.
  • Premiere Magazine - Online companion to the print magazine.
  • Right Turn Clyde - An independent film zine with the ideals that Hollywood blockbusters tend to be sub par.
  • Screen International - Online version of the print magazine. Film industry news, movie reviews, feature articles, festival and awards coverage, production news, and special reports.
  • Slant Magazine - Zine featuring reviews and articles about film and music.
  • Total Film - Online version of the print magazine with news, weekly box office round-ups, release schedules for theaters, DVD, and Blu-ray, reviews, previews, trailers and clips, feature articles, contests, monthly free screenings in the UK, and forum.
  • Trauma Magazine - European horror, science fiction, and fantasy film magazine. Interviews, reviews, contests, screenplays, trivia, forum, and links.
  • VideoVista - Film-related articles and interviews, reviews of video and DVD releases, and competitions.
