Publications

Ace Camera Photography Magazine - Has an international focus, publishing articles and photography from contributors around the world. Topics include new product reviews, how-to articles and photo-illustrated stories.

Ag magazine - Journal featuring portfolios, critical writing, advice on photography technology, fine art printing and alternative processes.Published quarterly.

Amateur Photographer Magazine - For buying and selling cameras, dealer listings, exhibition previews, book reviews, photo competitions, Q&As, top photographers, readers' pictures and digital imaging.

Aperture - The not-for-profit arts institution dedicated to advancing fine photography, publishing a magazine and books, presenting a diverse selection of photographs and texts offering perspectives on current ideas in photography.

BlindSpot - Art photography magazine published three times a year. Subscription information as well as artist information and print editions for sale.

British Journal of Photography Online - Presents news, galleries, job listings, and a discussion forum.

Capricious - Based in New York featuring emerging photographic talent from all over the world.

European Photography - Brief introduction to the magazine and the publisher. Subscriptions, prints for sale, forums, and links.

Gomma - Showcases international photographers.

Hoboken Almanac - A periodical dedicated to photography, including interviews, articles, and resource links.

Inscape - Offers a mission statement, samples of articles, and subscription information.

Kit & Caboodle - A selection of reader submitted photography and links to other photography magazines and photographers.

Leica Fotografie International - Covers Leica cameras, photography techniques and well-known artists who work with Leica equipment.

Lens Culture - International contemporary photography: documentary, fine art, photojournalism, poetic, abstract, and street photography. Essays, criticism, photo book reviews, audio interviews with photographers. Extensive archives.

LensWork - Focuses on photography and the creative process, with articles, interviews and portfolios.

Outdoor Photographer - Offers tips, camera and equipment reviews, and articles from the print magazine.

Pentax User Magazine - Table of contents, backissue and subscription details. Includes Pentax galleries, news, forums, classifieds, and a price list.

Photo District News - A resource for professional photographers. Includes subscription information, photography news, a gallery, classifieds, contests, and a directory.

Photo Insider - Subscription and advertising information as well as a feature article.

Photo Life - Canadian publication with photography news, a gallery, and a question and answer forum. [English and French]

Photo Techniques - Professional techniques for the darkroom, black and white, color, and alternative processes.

Photo Trip USA - Features landscape photographers with an interest in the American West. [English, German, French, and Japanese]

Photographer's Forum - Quarterly photography publication for the professional photographer. Subscription information, contents of the current issue, and contests.

Photography in New York/International - Bi-monthly guide to fine art photography exhibits, dealers, and auctions. Includes a directory of services and a photographer's index.

Photography now - Quarterly publication and international online guide for photography and Video art.

Photomag - Includes equipment reviews, techniques, photo assessments, competitions, news and information.

PhotoMedia - Includes,editorials, industry news, a featured photographer, photography tips and subscription information. Published 3 times a year.

Photoworks Magazine - Forum for British and International photography, showcasing established artists and emerging talents alongside reviews and critical writing on the medium. Published twice a year in April and October and distributed internationally.

Popular Photography - Offers product reviews, forums, how to's, and features on photography. Also includes subscription information.

Portfolio - The catalogue of contemporary photography in Britain. Contains details of the back issues of the publication, subscription information, an artists' index, and a search facility to locate the name of writers or topics of interest.

Professional Photographer Magazine - Photographic and digital imaging equipment reviews, company updates, business management information, and profiles of photographers.

The Rangefinder - Provides photography instruction, advice, equipment reviews, discussions, an industry directory, and classifieds.

SeeSaw Magazine - International contemporary photrography, found photography, photo-related essays, and interviews.

Shots - Member-supported journal of black and white photography. Current issue, submission information and subscripition information.

Shutterbug - For photo enthusiasts and working professionals. Provides classifieds, industry updates, product previews, test reports, tips, links, how-to projects, and highlights of featured articles.

Source - Contemporary photography in Britain and Ireland. Includes the editorial page and contents of the current issue. Also includes an archive of articles, subscription information, a gallery of artists and links.

View Camera - A bi-monthly publication dedicated to the art and craft of large format photography. Includes an overview of the current issue, subscription information, classifieds, submission guidelines, books of interest, and workshops.

Zoom - Europe's fine photography magazine. Full portfolios, national focus articles, international exhibition calendar.
