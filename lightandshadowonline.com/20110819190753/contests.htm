Contests

Australian Digital Photo Competitions - Monthly photographic competitions for Australian photographers.

Austrian Super Circuit - Competition based in Hasselblad, Austria. Top prizes are cameras and about $30,000 US dollars. Includes a list of previous contest winners, photos of previous top winners and contact information.

BestFoto.com - Digital photo of the day contest. Includes photo of the month, galleries, and archives.

Bethesda International Photography Competition - An opportunity for photographers to showcase work in one the region's commercial galleries.

The Brush Art Gallery and Studios - Has a black and white photo contest.

Canadian International Circuit of Photography - Sponsor of a series of photo contests in eastern Canada. Discussed are locations, judges, and other sponsors. Includes a gallery of past contest winners' work.

CDS/Honickman First Book Prize in Photography - Biennial prize of $3,000, inclusion in traveling exhibition, publication of book of photography.

Cohasset Colonials Photography Competition - The purpose of this competition is to illustrate the best aspects of Colonial design and American architecture.

Daily Awards Photography Contests - Daily, weekly, monthly and yearly photography contests for photographers of all skill levels.

Digital Photography Challenge - Weekly digital photography challenges with photograph feedback, forums, tutorials, and many other community features for all skill levels.

EPIC: The Environmentally Aware Photographic Image Competition - Annual contest for underwater photographers and videographers.

The Every Man Photo Contest - Features competition philosophy, award categories, rules and archive of previous winners.

FotoFanatic.com - Discuss, post photos and enter a photo contest with multiple categories and cash prizes.

FotoSurf - Monthly online photographic contest, with voting for the current entries and invitation to enter in future. Also portfolios, archives, forum and links. Also in Dutch.

Fourth Annual Fruit Shoot - Sponsored by the California Rare Fruit growers, looking for exotic shots of fruit.

Gorgeous Calendars - Also offers custom calendar and appointment book application with photographs of models.

Gracies Cafe International Christian Art Festival - Visual Art Festival encouraging spiritual growth through the use of right brain ministries.

International Photography Awards - Annual photography contest with a $10,000 grand prize, open to both professional and amateurs. Includes information about the judges, rules, online entry form and previous winners.

iPhotoContest - International annual and themed photo contests.

London Salon of Photography - Contains medal winners, acceptances for future Salons, portfolios of Salon members, entry details and a note on the history.

Photographer's Forum 20th Annual Spring Photography Contest - Photographer's Forum Magazine annual contest. Substantial cash prizes and publication offered to winning entries.

Photos-of-the-Year.com - Provides digital photography contest and discussion forum. Categories include people, nature, and digital stock.

PhoTournament - Allows members to participate in free photography and photo-art tournaments as judges or challengers. Includes archive, latest winners, FAQ and application.

Scottish International Salon of Photography - Open to all, accepts color, monochrome and slides in all categories. Prestigous international competition.

Shaker Workshops Photography Competitions - Competition in two categories: interior shots, exterior shots. Photographs must highlight Shaker architecture, Shaker furniture and accessories in real life.

Shifting Baselines Ocean Media Project - The project is running a short and sweet photography contest for images that display changes within our ocean and coastal environment.

Togher International Festival of Amateur Photography - Contest held annually in Cork, Ireland. Shows categories, entry form, prizes and awards, past entries and a note on the team behind the event.

Trophy Photos - Hunting and Fishing Photos - Hunting and Fishing photos.

Underwater Images Photo Competition - Annual international contest. Includes award prize list, sponsors, charitable benefactors and related organization links.

Venice International Photo Contest (VIPC) - [Venice, Italy] PSA approved photo competition and exhibition open to everyone. Themes and awards, conditions of entry, and calendar.

WHO Photo Contest "Images of Health and Disability" - The World Health Organization invites photographers around the world to submit photos in three categories: color, black & white, and digital photographs.
