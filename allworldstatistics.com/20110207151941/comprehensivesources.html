Comprehensive Sources

UNData - Keyword searchable database over over 50 million records from the United Nations and its agencies. Includes demographic, economic, education, health, environmental, agricultural, and technology indicators.
CIA World Factbook - Annual publication of country profiles and statistics.
FEDSTATS Agencies Page - Links to US federal agencies represented on the Interagency Council on Statistical Policy.
Infonation - United Nations site with statistical data on UN member states' geographies, economies, populations, and social indicators.
NationMaster.com - This free site compiles data from prominent international statistics sources like the CIA, UN and others and presents them graphically to allow easy comparisons of countries.
OFFSTATS: Official Statistics on the Web - Browse by country, region, or topic.
Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) - Agricultural, development, economic, educational, energy, environmental, financial, health, public sector, science and technology, and social policy statistics.
Statistical Resources on the Web - University of Michigan Document Center. Links to sites on Foreign Governments, Military, Political, Economic, Demographic, and Foreign Trade and Economics statistics, etc.
United Nations Statistics Division - Free access to selected portions of UN Statistical Publications such as the Demographics Yearbook and Statistics and Indicators on the World's Women.
US Census Bureau - The Census Bureau site has links to international population data, foreign trade statistics, and a commodity groupings database.
