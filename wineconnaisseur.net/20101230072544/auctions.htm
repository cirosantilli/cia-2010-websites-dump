Auctions

AuctionVine - Fine and rare wines for both dealers and collectors; includes a mock online auction for practise and an advanced search engine.

Brentwood Wine Company - Auction section of site features themed offerings, with various bidding options. Also wine shop with accessories, stemware and wine storage.

CellarExchange.com - A person to person online wine auction and exchange network. Features hammer price search, appraisals and a discussion forum.

The Chicago Wine Company - Features wine auctions as well as retail sales, recent hammer prices and events. Allows participation by phone, fax and e-mail.

Hospices de Beaune Wine Auction - Outlines the long history of this auction in Burgundy, France. Results of the November 19, 2000 event as well as the 1999 auction.

MagnumWines - Auction house of rare and sought-after wines including magnums. Pages for buyers and sellers, as well as a search feature.

Rare French Wines - Auctioning a bottle of Dom Perignon Millesime 1993. Registration required.

WhiskyAuction.Com - Bid online for single malt and other whiskies. Also online shopping.

The Wine Commune - Free person to person wine auctions. Also has a discussion forum.

WineBid.com - Buy or sell fine and rare wines. Indexed by type, vintage, appellation, producer, or current bid.
