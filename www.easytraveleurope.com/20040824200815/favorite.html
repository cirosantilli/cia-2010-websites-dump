The Beau-Rivage Palace.

Located in 10 acres of parkland, on the banks of Lake Geneva in Lausanne, the elegant belle époque buildings of the Beau-Rivage Palace command breathtaking views across the lake to the majestic French Alps beyond. Beau-Rivage Palace is today as resplendent as at its inauguration’s day and is one of Europe’s truly original resort hotels.

Village Maritalia.

At Peschici, one of the most charming localities of the Puglia coast, Village Maritalia has been built with the specific intent of blending in with and respecting the unique environment. The village consists of groups of single-story bungalows with private pergola garden. On-site there are two restaurants, boutiques, piano bar, swimming pool, tennis, archery, entertainment, and many other amenities.

Hotel Berghof.

Forget about the everyday life stress and enjoy into the family atmosphere of the Hotel Berghof. Zermatt is located in southern Switzerland at an altitude of 1600 meters and just a few hours from the international airports of Zurich, Geneva and Milano. Zermatt is easily accessible from western, northern or southern Europe. A vacation paradise, winter or summer.

Sporthotel Alpenland.

Sporthotel Alpenland is located in Maria Alm, a scenic village in-between gorgeous mountains. This four-star hotel is located smack in the middle of a perfect skiing and hiking area. Built in the chalet style, each vacation home has everything to make you feel comfortable: beautiful hotel rooms, suites and apartments, international atmosphere, balanced culinary art and a variety of recreational facilities.

Gålå Fjellgrend.

Gålå Fjellgrend is situated in the cultural center of Norway, close to the spectacular Winter Olympics area and within easy driving distance of fjord country and the glaciers. This all-year resort offers total relaxation and a variety of activities from downhill and cross-country skiing to bicycling, fishing, horseback riding, canoeing, and hill-walking. The activities are organized by Gålå Ski and Summer Arena. Nearby is the Gålå Høgfjellshotell og Hytter, renowned for its superb Norwegian cuisine. The cabins, built in traditional Norwegian style, are fully equipped to a very high standard.
