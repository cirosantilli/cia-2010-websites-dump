Mozart, mountains and Wiener schnitzel are among the first things that come to mind when one thinks about Austria. There is much more to this lovely country that is so rich in history, culture and of course cuisine.

Some of the world’s best ski resorts are in the Austrian Alps. But the alpine republic is also a great destination for summer vacations, for cultural trips or city tours.

Visit one of the great music festivals, indulge in the rich and superb cuisine and get enchanted by picture-book villages in front of landscapes that seem just too pretty to be real.

From Salzburg to the Italian border you will find a true vacation paradise that suits every taste. Ski in the Alps or spend a romantic weekend in front of the fireplace. The possibilities are endless.
