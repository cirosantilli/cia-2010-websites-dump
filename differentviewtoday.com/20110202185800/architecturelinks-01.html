Magazines and E-zines
  • Abitare - Subscription information, lists of back issues, and history. [in English and Italian]
  • Arbitat Views - Online architecture magazine featuring news, reviews, pictures and the arbitat futurewatch list of important buildings currently in design or under construction
  • ArchE - The world of architecture in competitions, theories, polemics, commerce and propaganda. A chronicle of architecture's footnotes and trivia.
  • Archinect - Aims to bring together designers from around the world to introduce new ideas from all disciplines. Features, news, views, image gallery and event calendar.
  • ArchiNed - An architectural platform collecting, linking and editing internet information on the design professions in the Netherlands. [in English and Dutch]
  • Archinnovations - Features the innovative directions in contemporary Architecture, as well as a look on innovators of the past who helped in shaping what architecture is today. Stories, articles, forums for discussion, and news of competitions and exhibitions.
  • Archis - A bi-monthly print magazine on architecture, city, and visual culture. Archive of feature articles. [Dutch and English]
  • The Architects Journal - A British independent architecture magazine with information on news, jobs, buildings, regulations, products, materials and events.
  • Architects Online - International webzine of architecture. Competitions, course curricula, directories and news. [content in Italian, German and English]
  • Architectural Design Sense - Digital magazine focussing on ideas for the home. Includes tours of exceptional homes from across North America: exteriors, interiors, pools and fittings.
  • Architectural Digest - International magazine of interior design. Introduction to content and how to subscribe.
  • Architectural Record - Online version of the print magazine, covering issues in architecture, professional directories, and recruitment listings.
  • The Architectural Review - Architecture magazine with a global perspective. Every month detailed and illustrated reviews of buildings and architectural projects are featured.
  • Architecture - Online version of the magazine of the same name. Coverage of architectural design, projects and products, industry news and trends. Searchable archive of past issues.
  • Architecture Lab - An online magazine dedicated to everything related to architecture, urban and sustainable design. Articles and archives.
  • Architecture Media - Publishes magazines on architecture in Australia, interiors, design, houses and building products. They are Architecture Australia, Houses, Artichoke, and Architectural Product News.
  • Architecture Week - Covers new buildings worldwide and a spectrum of design and technical issues.
  • Architecture-Page - Online magazine featuring architecture and product design.
  • ArchNewsNow - Online archive of an e-mailnewsletter. Links to architecture, design and construction stories, mostly from mainstream media.
  • Azure Magazine - A bi-monthly magazine focused on design, architecture, and interiors. Current issue and events listings.
  • BD - UK magazine Building Design's online presence, providing news for architects. Includes events, people and jobs.
  • Canadian Architect - Timely topics on architecture with a Canadian bent; international architecture news is also covered.
  • Competitions Magazine - Design competitions announcements for architecture, landscape architecture and urban planning.
  • Contract Magazine - Has a special focus on interior design and architecture for the corporate, retail, educational, hospitality, health care, entertainment, government, institutional, and performing arts markets.
  • Design Share - Journal of educational facility planning. Includes design awards and directory of architects.
  • DesignIntelligence - Bi-monthly report from the Design Futures Council. Includes research, commentary and management practices.
  • Domus - Global reporting on architecture, design, communication, art, web art, home furnishing and interior design. [in Italian and English]
  • Fabprefab - Focused on modernist prefabricated dwellings, featuring news and reviews of projects from around the world.
  • The Finnish Architectural Review - Features design, urban planning, interiors, housing and landscape. Not all articles are available online. [Finnish and English]
  • Five Foot Way Magazine - An architectural e-zine founded as a medium for the exploration and discourse of Asian architecture.
  • Form Magazine - Features emerging talents and precedent-setting work in the areas of architecture and design. Includes subscriptions and back issues [formerly known as LA Architect].
  • Katarxis - This web-zine presents and promotes traditional architecture and urbanism in the contemporary world.
  • Living Architecture - Online magazine covering Scandinavian design.
  • Log and ANY - Log is a journal for architecture and urbanism, and ANY is a bimonthly magazine, from the Anyone Corporation.
  • McDermott Publishing - Publishers for the UK building and construction industry. Titles include Building News, DABS, The Builder and The Architect.
  • Metropolis Magazine - Online version of the print magazine covering architecture, interior design, crafts, product and graphic design and urban planning.
  • Modern Building Services - Offers an editorial perspective covering not only traditional areas of building services but also seeking to unify the role of everyone in the industry across the entire building process, from inception to lifetime maintenance and refurbishment.
  • Naturalspace - Magazine features sustainable architectural projects from around the world and looks further to the natural world with articles on seasons, moods, sustainable lifestyles and processes.
  • OASE - Independent architectural journal (bilingual Dutch/English), published on behalf of the OASE Foundation. Archive of past issues.
  • Professional Lighting Design - Official magazine of the European Lighting Designers' Association and the International Association of Lighting Designers. Past issues and ordering.
  • Quaderns - Magazine on contemporary architecture, urban planning and arts. [English and Spanish]
  • Trends - Home and commercial design ideas, from kitchen design to architecture to home theatre trends.
  • U.S.Architecture - Features projects throughout the United States. Magazines are published by regions. Articles feature large projects both in the private and public sectors.
  • ViA Arquitectura - Quarterly review on particular aspects of recent international architecture. [in Spanish and English]
