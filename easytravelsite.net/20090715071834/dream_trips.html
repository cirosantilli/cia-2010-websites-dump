Do you know about this hotel?



Hydropolis

Is set up in Dubai. There are three important facets about the underwater hotel that it comprises a land station set up especially for the guests, a subway with a railway to transport the commuters and 220 hotel suites with a submarine leisure complex. One of the biggest luxury hotel constructions that have been taken up, the underwater project is set up over a total land area of 260 hectares.

Set up in Dubai, amidst the desert sands Hydropolis, the underwater hotel is the dream hotel of His Royal Highness, General Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the Crown Prince of Dubai. The development of the hotel project is being carried on the last beach property of the crown prince that was still not confiscated. One of the best hotel projects ever, the hotel project has been undertaken for the international community at large.

One of the luxurious hotel projects ever, Hydropolis, the underwater hotel appears as a fine blend of fantasy with the science of constructing homes. The name of a number of companies has come up with the undertaking of the construction of the underwater project. More than a hundred and fifty firms have currently come up in the project concern.

The original idea of the underwater hotel project came up when the supervisor professed a passion for the underwater and the seas. As it is believed that water is source of sustenance of the living beings on Earth, be it humans or even the Earth with more than 80% of water content.

In the architectural design of the hotel, we find the impression of humans and we find a correlation between the human physiology and building set up. The geometrical element is eight sided and inscribed in a circle and the ballroom at the centre has asymmetrical pathways, are flanked by ramps, elevators and staircases...
