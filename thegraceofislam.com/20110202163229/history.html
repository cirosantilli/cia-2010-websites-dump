History

  • Alkhazina - An interactive database for the study of Islamic Culture, particularly in the early centuries. Includes maps, articles, biographies and a timeline.
  • A Brief Chronology of Muslim History - Important dates in Muslim history, organised by century.
  • Gregorian -- Hijri Date Converter - Online conversion tool to convert between western and Muslim calendars; includes background information on the Hijri system.
  • Internet Islamic History Sourcebook - A categorized listing of links to aspects of the history of Islam; also includes translations of source texts, and articles.
  • Islam - An article on the history of Islam, from the early community, to the 20th century resurgence. Taken from the Encyclopedia of Politics and Religion, ed. Robert Wuthnow (1998).
  • Islam, 622 AD - present - A susbstantial and detailed perspective on the history of Islam, by Kelley L Ross, Los Angeles Valley College, California.
  • Islam, A World Civilization - A brief outline of the history of Islam.
  • Islam in the World - A collection of articles presenting the history and development of Islam in Africa, America, the Balkan states, China, Japan, Malaysia, and the USSR.
  • Islam on the Warpath - A lecture by Professor G Rempel (Western New England College, US) explores the expansion of Islam, and its contribution to science and culture.
  • Islamic Civilization - Historical review of the Muslim contribution to humanity and Islamic Civilization. Gives biographies of scientists and describes their contributions and influence on European thought and discoveries.
  • Islamic Civilization - Articles on aspects of the development of Islamic civilization, focussing on Asia and cultural contact between the Islamic East and the European West in Spain, Syria, and Sicily.
  • Islamic History in Arabia and Middle East - An extended essay on the history of Middle Eastern Islam, from the time of Mohammed to the 20th century.
  • The Islamic World to 1600 - Covers the 7th to the 17th centuries, including information about Islamic art; bibliography.
  • Muslimheritage.com - An interactive presentation by the Foundation for Science Technology and Civilisation (UK) explores Islamic achievements and contributions to science, technology and civilisation.
  • Muslims and Medical History - Articles about the history of Islamic medicine and the contributions of Muslim physicians (including Avicenna, Al-Razi, Al-Zahrawi, and Ibn Al-Nafis); also includes some primary sources.
  • PBS : Islam, Empire of Faith - A companion site to the PBS television series "Islam: Empire of Faith", which presented the history of Islam from the time of Muhammad to the Ottoman sultans. Includes educational resources, interactive timeline, and articles on Islam and Muslim culture.
  • What is Islam? - A brief history of Islam, and of the development of Muslim culture.
  • Internet Islamic History Sourcebook - texts and links to Islamic history.
  • Islam: Empire of Faith - PBS film about the faith, culture, innovation, and people of the Islamic empires.
  • Brief Chronology of Muslim History - a timline of events in the history of Islam from the 6th century to modern times.
  • Islamic Culture and the Medical Arts - from the National Library of Medicine.
  • Islamic World to 1600, The - outlines Muslim beliefs and practices, the history of the Islamic world from the 7th to the 17th centuries, and the expansion of the Islamic empires of Asia, Africa, and Europe.
  • History of Medieval Islam
  • History of Islam - provides chronology of Islam from 6th to 20th century.
  • Meccan Trade and the Rise of Islam - written by Patricia Crone.
  • Early Islamic Conquests - written by Fred Donner.
  • Islam In The Modern World
  • Islamic History in Arabia and Middle East - with sections on Islam in Spain, the Mongol and Mamluks, Ottomans, and the Crusaders.
  • Pages of Muslim History - featuring historical highlights divided into text chapters.
  • 15th Century Islamic History - offers a timeline.
