Acting: Chat Forums

  • AACT Online Forums - A selection of boards for the discussion of acting and topics related to the theatre.
  • Acting Only Club - Yahoo discussion group for actors to exchange information and gossip about working within the field of acting. Includes live chat, photos, links and profiles.
  • Actor Forums - A compact forum with acting as the main focus.
  • Actors Forum - Offers an interactive message board relating to acting classes, technique, management, movies, directing, producing and writing.
  • The Actor's Web Log - Discussion of modern acting theory and theatre practice, method acting, character acting, and directing.
  • Amdram - Amateur Theatre and Drama Forums - A forum discussing many areas of acting and related fields of interest.
  • Arts: Performing Arts: Acting: Chats and Forums - A forum discussing many areas of acting and related fields of interest.
  • Little Theatre Guild Message Board Forums - A collection of general boards for guild theatres.
