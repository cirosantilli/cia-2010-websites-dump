Magazines

  • Nature Photographer Magazine
    Offers you the opportunity to enhance your techniques through a variety of articles targeting all aspects of nature photography.
  • Outdoor Photographer
    When it comes to capturing nature's beauty at its best, you can't beat the photography tips from Outdoor Photographer. From reviews on photography equipment to the latest techniques, explore it all at the Outdoor Photographer website.
  • Digital Photographer
    Features subscription and advertising information, as well as a listing of the digital photography articles to be found in the printed version of the current issue.

    The Shutterbug magazine website provides classifieds, industry updates, product previews, test reports, tips, links, how-to projects, highlights of articles, and much more.
  • PCPhoto Magazine
    PC Photo shows you how to enjoy the exciting and affordable new world of computers and photography.
  • Amateur Photographer
    Amateur Photographer is the world’s oldest consumer weekly photographic magazine, first hitting the newsstands on October 10 1884.
  • PopPhoto
    The online home of Popular Photography and American Photo.
  • Nature's Best Photography
    Nature's Best Photography is a large format, quarterly publication that offers a showcase presentation of nature recorded by the world’s leading photographers, and naturalists.
  • Photo Life
    Website features articles, news, classifieds, back issues, subscription information, a newsletter, and more.
  • Digital Photographer
    On-line version of the British Magazine. It offers some article teasers and tutorial image downloads. Users can also upload their own portfolios.
  • JPG Magazine

  • Photo Techniques
    Information and articles on traditional and digital photographic techniques for serious amateurs and professional photographers alike.
  • PhotoReview Australia
    Features camera reviews, news, tips, guides, and an online camera recommender.
  • Lens Work
    Portfolios, essays and articles on photography and the creative process.
  • What Digital Camera

  • Rangefinder
    Provides photography instruction, advice, equipment reviews, discussions, an industry directory, and classifieds.
  • Digital Camera

  • D-Photo
    Digital photography guide with camera reviews and articles, comprehensive technique features, news and photography competitions.
