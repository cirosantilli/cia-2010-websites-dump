Science and Environment

Caribbean Agricultural Research and Development Institute - Technical assistance, development and technology transfer in agricultural sciences. Based in St. Augustine, Trinidad.

Caribbean Conservation Association - Organization that deals with environment and sustainable living issues. Provides objectives, mission statement, board members, activities and programs, publications, membership and contact information.

EcoLogic Development Fund - US organization conserving threatened wildlife and wildlands by advancing community-based development and natural resource management in Mexico and Central America. Includes project descriptions.

Centres of Plant Diversity - Description of climate, geology, and wildlife of Central America, with links to more detailed information on key areas.

North American Waterfowl Population Surveys - Information about U.S.-Canada-Mexico cooperative efforts to monitor populations and breeding habitats for ducks, geese and swans.

United Plant Savers - Works to protect native medicinal plants of the United States and Canada and their native habitat while ensuring an abundant renewable supply of medicinal plants for future generations. Forum and newsletters.

Amazon Watch - Works with indigenous and environmental organizations in the Amazon Basin to defend the environment and advance indigenous rights in the face of large-scale industrial development. Provides news, campaigns, resources and contacts.
