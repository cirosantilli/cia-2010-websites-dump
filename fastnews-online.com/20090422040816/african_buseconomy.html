African Business - Monthly business magazine about Africa provides current issue's articles plus breaking news and special reports. From IC Publications.

Review of African Political Economy - Journal focussing on understanding, and challenging the causes, of underdevelopment in Africa . Abstracts, contents, subscription information.

World News Network - Africa Business Daily - Headline links to news and analysis on about African business, finance and economic issues.

Africa Business Pages - Offers ways to promote products in African countries through direct contacts with business counterparts there.

Africa Confidential - Information service covering economic and political issues. Has a subscription service for full issues.

Emerging Africa Infrastructure Fund - Represents a new financing approach for the long term alleviation of poverty in sub-Saharan Africa.

Simba Trade - Online portal specializing in African products, manufacturers, factories, business, suppliers, exporters, and trade opportunities.

MBendi - Business Directory - African business directory includes companies, news, publications, events, organizations, tenders and employment. Searchable by name or type of organization, industry or location.
