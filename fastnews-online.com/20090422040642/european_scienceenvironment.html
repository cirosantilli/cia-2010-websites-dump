Science and Environment

Coastal Guide to Europe - Offers information to the visitor of coastal areas in Europe. Topics include history of the landscape, whale and dolphin watching, other wildlife, nature and recreation areas and cultural heritage (historical cities, buildings and museums).

Community Research and Development Information Service - Cordis provides access to a wide range of information on EU research and innovation development activities.

European Centre for Nature Conservation - Purpose is to further European nature conservation by bridging gap between science and policy. ECNC has become a renowned expertise centre offering support to the development, review and implementation of European nature conservation.

European Environment Agency - Provides information for sound and effective policies to protect the environment and support sustainable development.

Organic Europe - An overview of organic farming in 25 European countries.
