Not even Washington D.C.'s Smithsonians can offer a more diverse collection of museums and exhibits than the ones found in New York City.

NYC has its famous art museums like the Guggenheim and the stunning Metropolitan Museum of Art. Cultural specific exhibits like the Museum of Jewish Heritage and the Hispanic Society of America are available as well as New York's most famous museum, the American Museum of Natural History. You can also check out NYC's newest edition all the way from London, the world famous Madame Tussaud's Wax Museum.

Buy a copy of Frommers for New York. It is an invaluable resource to have while hiking the city and seeking the educational wonders New York's museums have to offer.
