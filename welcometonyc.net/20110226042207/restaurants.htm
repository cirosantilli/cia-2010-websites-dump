On my last trip to New York I was amazed at the sheer volume of restaurants the city had to offer a hungry tourist. It was almost too much to take in and it definitely made it close to impossible to choose one for every meal. But when the choice was made I was rarely disappointed in the cuisine.

New York's diverse cultural tapestry has provided native New Yorkers and the millions of tourists that visit it every year a chance to taste the foods of the world. Authentic Chinese, Italian, Greek, Caribbean, Indian and dozens of other tasty choices await you!

I'd like to recommend before going to NYC, buy a copy of Frommers for New York. It is an invaluable resource to have while hiking the city and checking out restaurants. It's also necessary to make reservations well in advance for some of the nicer eateries.
