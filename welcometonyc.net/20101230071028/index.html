What kind of things do you look for when planning a vacation? Good food? Lots of things to see? Lots of things to do? Cultural diversity and good entertainment?

If you answered yes to any of those or all of them then there is no better place to go than the greatest city in the world - New York.

TOURISM
When you think of live theater you think Broadway first. Come see the classic musicals like Jersey Boys and Chicago or the newest smash hit Mamma Mia!!. Or if musical theater isn't your thing catch a play like The Graduate or Dinner at Eight...or perhaps the Opera or the NYC Ballet located at the Lincoln Center.

If you're looking for live music NYC offers everything from jazz to heavy metal in hundreds of live music clubs scattered around the city. Famous artists such as The Ramones and Bon Jovi got their start playing in the small clubs that Gotham is famous for.

And of course New York is famous for its landscape. The skyscrapers, Statue of Liberty, Ellis Island and Central Park are a few of the many attractions the city has to offer.

FOOD
Because of its rich tradition of cultural diversity and big appettite the Big Apple has a vast selection of first class restaurants, offering delicacies from all over the world. From the new style Japanese dishes at Nobu to fine Czarist Russian cuisine at The Firebird Restaurant, NYC's vast expanse of eateries has something for everyone.

MUSEUMS
New York is the United States' cultural mecca, offering some of the most breath-taking museums, theater, and music anywhere. New York's museum community includes the Metropolitan Museum of Art and its stunning collection of masterpieces which range from paintings to suits of armor. You can also visit the enormous American Museum of Natural History that features a huge collection of dinosaur skeletons and other wonders of the natural world, past and present.
