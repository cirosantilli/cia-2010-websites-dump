The world is becoming smaller and smaller everyday with the emergence of technology, yet so few people have actually seen the world outside their own country's borders. There is so much to see and experience in this vast world we live in, and The Tourist Trap wants to help you choose the perfect place to start, or continue, your journey to the 4 corners of the Earth.

Each page on The Tourist Trap will have featured links to online travel resources to help you find out about some of the most interesting, beautiful, mysterious and fun places to visit, as well as the most cost effective ways to get there.

Try a safari in Africa, or an eco-vacation in Costa Rica. Take a bus tour of London, a mule ride down the Grand Canyon and a cruise on the Amazon. You can find info on all that and more on The Tourist Trap.

Remember, think globally and enjoy all this wonderful world has to offer!
