Information Resources

  • Antique and decorative Oriental rugs and textiles - Antique tribal rugs and decorative carpets.
  • ASIAN ARTS On-line journal for the study and exhibition of the arts of Asia.
  • Brandon Oriental Rugs A nice introduction for newcomers to the rugs world.
  • Carpet Encyclopedia - Information about all aspects of care and collecting of oriental rugs. Multilingual.
  • Center for the Study of Ancient Textiles Site dedicated to archeological textiles, particularly clothing.
  • Golden Carpet Industries - Pakistan. Distributors of handknotted carpets and rugs, from wool. Links to the history, production and care for woollen carpets.
  • Guide to Hamadan Rugs - Notes on Oriental Rugs. Learn about Oriental rugs and carpets.
  • Islamic Carpets showing examples of knot patterns.
  • IslamicAart.com: Oriental Rugs - This Web presentation explains in detail the historical and cultural significance of Arabic Calligraphy. Very rich with information
  • Kilim Kilims and other textiles, with articles and a discussion forum.
  • Nomads | history of oriental rugs - Oriental rugs are frequently depicted in icons and paintings of both the western world as well as the oriental miniatures.
  • NonPlusUltra A commercial site that also includes articles, schedules of upcoming events, and other informational content.
  • Oriental/Persian Rugs Information
    Rug Information include History, Origin, Weavers, Loom, Knot, Design, Price Factors, and Protecting your Oriental Rugs.
  • Oriental Rug Gallery Online - We search the world for unique and beautiful oriental rugs and bring them here for you.
  • Oriental Rugs History. Excerpts from the book, Oriental Rugs, by John Kimberly Mumford, published 1900, Charles Scribner’s Sons, New York.
  • Oriental Rugs, The Connoisseur's Guide - Recommendations for identifying and evaluating oriental rugs and carpets.
  • Arthur T. Gregorian, Inc. website
  • Symmetry and Pattern: The Art of Oriental Carpets - In this online exhibit, the study of symmetry is used to analyze patterns in Oriental carpets. A joint project of The Textile Museum and The Math Forum.
  • Oldcarpet.com Persian rugs and carpets.
  • Rugman.com - Includes information on Persian and Oriental rug history, techniques of construction, a guide to rug buying, maintenance suggestions and a glossary of terms.
  • Textil Werkstatt Contemporary textiles with numerous links to textile related sites, including museums.
  • Tribal Art Forum  Turkotek-like site dedicated to tribal arts around the world.
  • Tribal Textiles Photographer/textile collector's record of her travels, mostly in southeast Asia, with a strong emphasis on tribal textiles and their use.
  • Tugra Beautiful site, full of information about Turkish calligraphic signatures.
  • Turkic Republics and Communities  Essentially a series of links, it is just what it says it is: "The most complete guide to the Turkic world." An almost overwhelming variety of information.
  • Turkmen Main Carpets  Iinformation rich CD-ROM produced by Kurt Munkacsi, David d'Huerle and Peter Saunders.
  • Vanishing Textiles Selections from an exhibition (in North Carolina State University’s Gallery of Art and Design) and book on ethnographic textiles.
  • World of Beduin Weaving  Informational site about the weavings of the Beduins of North Africa.
  • World of Tribal Arts On line version of a "Journal of the Art, History and Culture of Traditional People."

War Rugs

  • Afghan War Rugs Collector site on rugs woven following the Russian invasion of 1979.
  • Rugs of War - Australian Research Council Discovery Project to examine the emergence of distinctive and innovative iconography in the "war rugs" of Afghanistan from the time of Soviet occupation in 1979.

Educational Sites

  • Armenian Rugs and Carpets Scholarly essay on the history and significance of rugs and carpets in Armenia.
  • Catalhoyuk Excavations Site devoted to Turkish archeological site.
  • DOBAG Project: Australia Home page of the Australian office of the pioneering project undertaken to reestablish traditional weaving methods in Turkey.
  • Oriental Rug Notes by Barry O'Connell - Notes on Oriental Rugs and Carpets. Thousands of pages of information on the Persian carpet, the Oriental Rug, the Oriental Carpet and more.
  • Richard Farber  Composer, conductor, playright, textile collector invites visitors to get to know him and his work. 
  • Richard E. Wright Research Reports A scholar's notes on the nature and history of textiles.  Many images.
  • RugLore The late Sam Gorden's site, dedicated to the viewpoint that aesthetics is what rugs are all about. Much useful advice for new and experienced collectors.
  • Silk Road Foundation - Nonprofit educational foundation. Website is extremely rich in information about the Silk Road, the cultures along it, and the history.
  • Woven Souls Jaina Mishra's site, with articles and exhibitions of textile art, mostly from the Indian subcontinent.
