Auction Houses

  • Aspire Auctions, Inc. - Aspire Auctions is an online auction house specializing in art, antiques and oriental carpets.
  • Bonhams - Auctioneers of fine art, antiques, collectables and motor cars.
  • Bonhams & Butterfields - Specializes in the appraisal and sale of fine art, rugs and carpets, antiques and decorative objects.
  • Bruun Rasmussen Auctions - Web site for Bruun Rasmussen Auctions.
  • Bukowski Auktioner - Bukowskis has four main auctions per year. Two of them are international auctions with paintings, antique furniture, carpets, silver and jewellery etc. English and Swedish versions.
  • Christie's - Premier auction house.
  • Galerie Koller - Auction house in Zurich and Geneva for antiques, pictures, watches, furniture and carpets.
  • Grogan & Company - Fine art auctioneers and appraisers.
  • Nagel Auctions - Auctioneers for fine arts and antiques. Specialist rugs and carpets sale. Includes an art sale price database with more than 1600 rugs and carpets. English and German versions.
  • Rippon Boswell - Specialist auctioneers for antique carpets and textiles. At least four auctions each year, usually in March, May, September and November. English and German versions.
  • Skinner - Auctioneers and appraisers of antiques and fine art with galleries in Boston and Bolton. Oriental rugs and carpets auctions normally two times each year.
  • Sotheby's - Premier auction house for fine art, antiques, books, jewelry, rugs & carpets and other collectible memorabilia
  • Van Ham Kunst Auktionen - Fine art auction house for Paintings, Sculptures, Modern art, Furniture, Silver, Jewelry, Tapestries and Rugs & Carpets. English and German versions.
